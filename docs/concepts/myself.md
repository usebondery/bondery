# Myself

Every Bondery account has one special contact marked as **myself** — your own profile inside the app. It uses the same fields as any other person (name, avatar, phones, emails, socials) but represents you, not someone in your network.

## What it is used for

- **Sharing yourself** — when you export or share your contact details, Bondery reads from your Myself profile
- **Relationships** — the "yourself" side of relationship links points at this contact
- **vCard export** — download your own `.vcf` from the **Actions** menu on the Myself page

## How to access it

1. Click your **name / avatar** at the bottom of the left sidebar
2. Go to **Settings → Profile**

## Keeping it up to date

Myself is a regular contact under the hood. Edit name, channels, and avatar the same way you would for anyone else — changes apply immediately when you share or export.
