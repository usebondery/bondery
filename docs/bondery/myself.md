# Myself — Your profile contact

The **Myself** page is your personal contact card inside Bondery. It represents you — your name, avatar, contact details, and social links — and serves as the foundation for sharing yourself with others.

## What is it used for?

When you share a contact externally (for example, sending someone a vCard or sharing your contact details via a link), Bondery uses the information stored in your **Myself** profile. This includes:

- **Name** — your first, middle, and last name as others will see it
- **Avatar / profile photo** — the photo shown on your shared contact card
- **Phone numbers, email addresses, and social links** — any channels you've added to your profile

## Generating and sharing your own vCard

From the **Myself** page you can download your own vCard (`.vcf` file) using the **Actions** menu. This lets you:

- Share your contact details with someone who doesn't use Bondery
- Import yourself into another contacts app
- Send a pre-filled contact card via email or messaging

## Sharing yourself

Using the **Share** action, you can generate a shareable link or export your contact so others can add you to their contacts apps.

## How to access it

There are two ways to open your Myself profile:

1. Click your **name / avatar card** at the bottom of the left sidebar
2. Go to **Settings → Profile** and click your profile card

## Keeping it up to date

Your Myself profile is a regular Bondery contact. You can edit your name, add phone numbers, and update your avatar the same way you'd update any contact — all changes are reflected immediately when you share yourself.
