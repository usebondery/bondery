# 😎 Getting started

This guide walks you through setting up your Bondery account and getting the most out of it from day one.

## 1. Create an account

Go to [app.usebondery.com](https://app.usebondery.com) and sign up with your GitHub or LinkedIn account. Both options are available on the sign-in page.

{% hint style="info" %}
**Why no email and password?**
Bondery uses OAuth-only sign-in, meaning we never store your password. This means we cannot be breached for passwords, you benefit from your provider's two-factor authentication automatically, and you have one less credential to manage. Your chosen provider (GitHub or LinkedIn) handles all authentication.
{% endhint %}

## 2. Add your first contact

Once you're in, the first thing to do is add a person to your network.

1. Click **Add contact** (or the **+** button in the People section)
2. Fill in their name — everything else is optional
3. Add any details you know: phone, email, social links, location, or a note
4. Hit **Save**

You can always come back and add more details later. Even a name-only contact is useful.

## 3. Organize with groups

Groups let you segment your contacts into categories that make sense to you — "Close friends", "Work", "Family", or anything else.

1. Go to **Groups** in the sidebar
2. Click **New group**, give it a name and optionally an emoji or color
3. Open any contact and assign them to one or more groups

## 4. Log an interaction

Bondery tracks when you last spoke to someone. After a call, meeting, or coffee, log it:

1. Open a contact
2. Go to their **Interactions** tab
3. Click **Add interaction**, pick a type (call, meeting, coffee…), set the date, and save

The contact's **Last interaction** date updates automatically, making it easy to spot who you haven't been in touch with for a while.

## 5. Set a reminder

Never miss a birthday or anniversary again:

1. Open a contact
2. Go to the **Reminders** tab
3. Click **Add important date**, choose the event type (birthday, anniversary, etc.), enter the date, and set how many days before you want to be notified

Bondery will send you a digest email on the right day. You can configure what time you receive reminders in your **Account settings**.

## 6. Import contacts

There are two ways to bulk-import contacts:

**From LinkedIn or Instagram while browsing** — use the [Chrome Extension](chrome-extension.md) to capture a profile in one click as you visit it.

**From a LinkedIn or Instagram data export** — if you want to import your full contact list at once, go to **Settings → Data management**, click **LinkedIn import** or **Instagram import**, and follow the in-app guide from there.
